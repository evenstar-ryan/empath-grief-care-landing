Grief: Body, Mind, and Soul
Grief impacts our entire person. Join us as we discuss how to care for all aspects of self during grief and loss. *Note: This is a repeat of the seminar from fall 2025

Safety, Scams and Stepping Out
Navigating the world after loss can be tricky. The seminar will focus on safety concerns, including how to protect yourself from common scams. There will be a section at the end for those interested in moving forward with dating (if not interested, you can leave before this section starts). *This will also be presented in person at the Sarasota/Rand office location.
February 27, 2025 at Noon
Grief and Boundaries
Discover ways to set healthy boundaries that support your health and wellbeing during times of grief and loss.
March 13, 2026 at 10:30 AM
Using Art, Music and Movement to Navigate Grief
Art, music and movement are tools that can help us navigate grief. Join us to learn how to utilize these tools. *Note: Repeat of fall 2025 seminar
March 27, 2026 at 10:30 AM
Grief and Relationships: Building Social Support
During grief connecting with others can be challenging. Join us in exploring ways to build social support to help navigate your grief journey. *Note: Repeat of fall 2025 seminar
April 10, 2026 at Noon
Grief and Hope
During loss, hope can feel, and be, elusive. But hope is more than wishful thinking and love is stronger than our pain and loss. Join us as we discuss hope.

Special Events

Safety, Scams and Stepping Out (Virtual & In Person)

Join us in a safe space to discuss safety concerns often associated with living alone. Information will be shared on how to avoid scams, including financial and romantic scams. The final portion of the event focuses on dating after loss. Individuals adjusting to living alone and those moving through spousal loss who may be considering dating again are encouraged to attend.

*Participants who prefer not to attend the dating portion may leave before that segment begins.
